travis amiel: multidisciplinary artist

Hi it's me, Travis.

As an artist, I tinker with different mediums and genres in performances (e.g. puppetry, movement, stand-up, art-in-action) and digitally (i.e. web / graphic design). My projects investigate queerness, pretentiousness, shame, convenience, environmentalism, and commons/cooperative-based organising.

Throughout my life, I've used my adeptness with technology to set up systems, troubleshoot problems, and empower individual success with various platforms.

I live in New York City. If you'd like to reach me, email travis@travisamiel.com
